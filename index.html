Our Story

In Politeknik Negeri Medan, Hamdi and his friends established "CampusBlend" Coffee Shop. With its cozy design and diverse coffee menu, the place has become a gathering spot for the entire campus community. CampusBlend is not just a place to enjoy coffee, but also a space for creativity, sharing, and growth. In every cup of coffee, there is a spirit to pursue dreams and inspire one another. It serves as a symbol of unity on campus, spreading the aroma of friendship and the zest for learning in every corner. Through CampusBlend, students and staff can strengthen relationships and foster interdisciplinary collaboration.
